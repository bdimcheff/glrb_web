Venue

The Great Lakes Ruby Bash will be held on the campus of the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, Michigan on October 11th, 2008. All of the talks will be presented in Forum Hall in Atrium 4 and Atrium 5 of Palmer Commons located on central campus.

Please bring a CAT5/6 cable to get network access. Each seat will have its own power outlet and ethernet drop.

Directions

Address: 100 Washtenaw Avenue, Ann Arbor, MI 48109
Event map
